Hi, I’m Sean Meng — a neuroscientist in training at Harvard. I study how neural circuits for reward and cognitive flexibility form the scaffolding that underlies the ways we adapt, decide, and interact with the world.

I believe neuroscience is an extension of empathy: to understand the brain is to begin translating realities not our own.
